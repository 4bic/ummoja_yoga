Yoga for All — mind and body

Hello, we at Ummoja Yoga, believe in working with integrity on physical, spiritual and emotional dimensions of practice. We're really good at it.

Yoga incorporates a wide variety of bodily positions into regular exercises, strengthening and toning muscles that would otherwise go unnoticed in traditional workouts.

Yoga Foundations

We've designed our programs to help you cultivate balance, peace and poise. We train you to use the power tools of breath movement and discipline to get clear about what you want. You get to practice mindfully and regularly so that you find the energy you need to rise to all occasions.

Yoga for Scoliosis

Yoga for Scoliosis is an all levels yoga practice with a focus on the spine. It is also for anyone seeking to improve their posture and feel good! We use this practice to tune into your breath and tend to your mental head space.

Power & Weight Loss

Yoga break builds strength in the core, glutes, arms and legs. Per your request, we have a stress free 20 minutes power yoga that is centered around your breath and mindful movement.

Meditation (Dhyaan)

Cultivate a Meditations practice to help balance and restore both the physical and emotional body. Tend to your nervous system through mindful yoga. Connect your brain and body in a powerful way. With a focus on the breath this yoga practice invites you to be kind, accepting and heal naturally.
